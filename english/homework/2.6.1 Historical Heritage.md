# 2.6.1 Historical Heritage

### Task 1

1. Her generous donation will contribute greatly to the success of the charity event.
    - The success of the charity event will be contributed to greatly by her generous donation.
2. They've devised a plan for the area to undergo a makeover.
    - A plan has been devised for the area to undergo a makeover.
3. The family decided to abandon their cosy flat in the city and move to a new house in the suburbs.
    - A decision was made by the family to abandon their cosy flat in the city and move to a new house in the suburbs.
4. The scientists have been looking for the signs of life on Mars for decades.
    - Signs of life on Mars have been looked for by scientists for decades.
5. He has never complimented on Judy's excellent German.
    - Judy's excellent German has never been complimented on by him.
6. People often perceive a science degree and artistic interests as incompatible.
    - A science degree and artistic interests are often perceived by people as incompatible.
7. The process of gentrification transformed the once run-down neighbourhood into a trendy and upscale area.
    - The once run-down neighbourhood has been transformed by the process of gentrification into a trendy and upscale area.
8. Nothing would induce me to take the job.
    - I would not be induced to take the job by anything.
9. I don't like it when colleagues tell me how to settle arguments with clients.
    - It is disliked by me when colleagues tell me how to settle arguments with clients.
10. Snow forced many drivers to abandon their vehicles.
    - Many drivers were forced by snow to abandon their vehicles.
11. The proliferation of cell phones has changed the way we communicate.
    - The way we communicate has been changed by the proliferation of cell phones.
12. The local government appreciates the efforts of the volunteers at the football match.
    - The efforts of the volunteers at the football match are appreciated by the local government.
