# 4.2.1 Arts

### Task 1

1. The Bauhaus students **were taken aback** when the school was closed after 14 years of work.
2. Surprisingly, the sales of the Cesca chair **picked up steam** in the spring.
3. But what wealth does is **drive up** the price of certain important things.
4. The Bauhaus’ legacy has become **ubiquitous** all over the world.
5. Some artists put a special **emphasis** on colours in their paintings.
6. Gropius was a conservative who **advocated** fewer government controls on the development of the school.
7. This was the first (and the last) time that Meyer dared to **defy** the National Socialists.
8. The latest book about Andy Warhol had startling **disclosures** about his private life.
#### Explanation:
- **take aback → were taken aback** (past passive, expressing surprise)
- **pick up → picked up steam** (past tense, meaning "gained momentum")
- **drive up** (infinitive, meaning "increase prices")
- **ubiquitous** (adjective, meaning "found everywhere")
- **emphasis** (noun, "special attention")
- **advocate → advocated** (past tense, "supported")
- **defy → defy** (base form after "to dare to")
- **disclosure → disclosures** (plural noun, "revealed secrets")

### Task 4

1. "home-on-the-farm demeanor"
    - Describes Lorna's practical, down-to-earth personality typical of someone raised on a farm
    - Suggests straightforward, no-nonsense attitude common in rural upbringing
2. "an impish smile"
    - A playful, mischievous, or somewhat naughty smile
    - Suggests someone who has a fun-loving or slightly mischievous personality
3. "thirty-odd years"
    - Approximately thirty years, give or take a few years
    - The "odd" here means additional, unspecified number of years beyond thirty
4. "almond-shaped eyes"
    - Eyes with an elongated shape resembling an almond
    - A common physical characteristic in Asian populations
    - Used here to describe the distinctive eye shape of Bhutanese people
5. "varying hue but similar cry"
    - A poetic way to describe European colonizers
    - "varying hue" refers to their different nationalities/skin colors
    - "similar cry" suggests they all had the same goal/motivation (colonization)
6. "sign an outrageous treaty"
    - Refers to a historically unfair treaty Ashley Eden was forced to sign
    - "outrageous" implies the terms were extremely unreasonable and unfavorable
    - Context shows this was done under duress (after being physically harassed)
